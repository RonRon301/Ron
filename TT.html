Essential Tips for Your Hong Kong Trip

Packing Essentials

Carry lightweight clothing, comfortable shoes, and a raincoat as Hong Kong can be humid and rainy.

Use an Octopus Card

The Octopus Card is a convenient way to pay for public transport, convenience stores, and more.

Stay Connected

Buy a local SIM card or portable Wi-Fi device for affordable and reliable internet access.

Learn Basic Etiquette

Be respectful in temples, avoid loud conversations, and familiarize yourself with basic Cantonese phrases.

Public Transportation

Use the MTR, buses, and trams for efficient and affordable travel around the city.
