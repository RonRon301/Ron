Explore the Flavors of Hong Kong

Dim Sum

A quintessential Hong Kong experience, featuring steamed dumplings, buns, and other small bites served in bamboo baskets.

Egg Tarts

Flaky pastry filled with sweet egg custard, a popular dessert often found in bakeries across Hong Kong.

Wonton Noodles

Thin egg noodles served in a flavorful broth, topped with plump shrimp wontons, a must-try comfort dish.

Roast Goose

Crispy-skinned roast goose marinated with secret spices, served with plum sauce.

Hong Kong Milk Tea

A creamy and rich tea made with evaporated milk, a staple beverage in cha chaan tengs (tea restaurants).
